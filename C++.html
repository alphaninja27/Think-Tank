Get Started With C++

Starting out your coding journey can be a daunting process considering there is a plethora of resources that are available on the internet . Hence it becomes imperative to know the reasons behind learning a particular language, in this case C++, the resources to learn from and also the resources to use in order to practice questions and getting unstuck.
That's why we at Dissemination.co have come to your rescue by stating the reasons why C++ should be your First Programming language and also have curated the resources in order to make your C++ journey as bump free as possible.

1. WHY C++ ?

  • Allows you to get a deeper understanding of programming and its basics as it builds from ground up
  • Is the Preferred language for DSA which is essential for cracking Placement Interviews
  • Is widely Used for Game Dev
  • Is easier to switch to any language after mastering C++ than vice versa
